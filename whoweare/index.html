Evolution in an icon

To evolve means to improve to be able to respond to new challenges. To accommodate the future and be a part of TIM is also renewed in the brand. Since its inception, more than 20 years ago, its logo was a sign of modernity and dynamism: a promise of projects and new horizons. Today continues to be a symbol of change, combining the expertise of two realities: The red trigram of Telecom Italia, the intense blue of the TIM brand and a T to merge these two worlds. The synthesis of a great legacy and a strong orientation to the future. The result is a simple and instant icon: evolved, for evolved times.
