Business

Proximity, safety and quality keywords of the business model. With the reorganization of the wholesale function and an investment of 120 million Euros and the state launched a new model of equivalence to further strengthen the Equal access to the fixed network by Telecom Italy and ALL operators.
Our key markets are Italy and Brazil. The activities are followed by business units dedicated: the Domestic Business Unit - voice and data services, from fixed and mobile networks for final customers (retail) and other operators (wholesale), international wholesale services (Telecom Italy Sparkle) and IT solutions (Olivetti) -, the Brazil Business Unit (TIM Brasil). Our organization, in line with the evolution of the business model of industry and market trends and technology, is turning towards a "Digital Telco Platform & Company" model. A model based on innovative infrastructure and excellent customer service and points to the spread of services and premium digital content offered on a customizable platform and usable anywhere and on qualsisasi device. The commercial presence of retail customers - that is private (individual customers and households) and business customers - refers to these organizational drivers: ensure the accountability of end to end processes of marketing, sales and after sales of each customer segment, foster the development of integrated "customer centric" and focusing on the digital market services create synergies between the commercial channels, to increase the efficiency and effectiveness of the multi-channel model encourage the development of "platform-based" services by expanding the product portfolio of TIM pay attention to multinational customers, for the development of international connectivity. To this end, the activities on the domestic market are structured in these main functions:

Consumer & Small Enterprise Market: this function has a responsibility to ensure the operating margin from the operations to the consumer and small business segments, maximizing revenues, profitability and market share through the offer, marketing plans, business communication, the marketing of products / services, caring activities, operating credit and the administrative management of customers. The aim is to increase customer satisfaction and, at the same time, optimizing the overall cost of the service. This function refers, from an organizational point of view, the Persidera company, formed by the merger of Telecom Italy Media Broadcasting and Network A.

ICT Solutions & Service Platforms: with the responsibility of ensuring the proveiente operating margin for the activity towards the top segments, Strategic, Large, Medium Enterprise and Public Administration, maximizing revenues, profitability and market share through the offer, plans to marketing and sales communications. It also ensures the design of ICT solutions, the marketing of products / services, post-sales activities, customer care and the responsibility of credit management. In this function comprises, from an organizational standpoint, Olivetti and Telecom Italy Sparkle.

International Wholesale Services: refers to Telecom Italy Sparkle activities that operates in the market for international voice, data and Internet for operators of fixed and mobile telecommunications, ISPs and multinational companies through its own networks in the European, Mediterranean and South America.

Wholesale: the function, created in November 2015, bringing together the Open Access and National Wholesale Services functions. This organizational change aims to strengthen ulteriano the efficiency and effectiveness of the processes of activation and maintenance of wholesale access services to the fixed network of Telecom Italy provided to their sales departments and the OLOs, putting on the same plane, in the employ of the same charge, the commercaili divisions of Telecom Italy and the OLOs. The Wholesale function thus has the following responsibilities: to ensure the wholesale business development, innovation, the definition of supply and marketing of its products and services; ensure end-to-end process delivery and assurance services to retail and wholesale customers, consistent with an effective of the access network infrastructure management, and following the principles of non-discrimination between internal and external activities; ensure the overall governance and monitoring end-to-end performance and process indicators, with the aim of maximizing the level of quality of services provided and ensure fairness and transparency.

Technology: it is responsible for ensuring the technological innovation and development processes, engineering, building and operation of network infrastructure.
